How GSBurnCals Works

Exercise, burn calories, and you raise your Fuelscore! Input your FuelBand data to earn points and gain status. Use your points to play the game, tip the scale, and win rewards!

Get started on Fri, Feb 15th by uploading you FuelBand data from the 14th. You must make your last upload and play out all your points before the program ends at 12pm on Wednesday March 13th.

FuelBand

What kinds of activity does the FuelBand track?

The FuelBand measures acceleration, so any activity involving arm movement will increase your FuelScore. The band tracks aerobic activities like running, walking, dancing, and basketball effectively, but is less suited for resistance workouts, like weight lifting and yoga. The FuelBand is not optimized to track bicycle workouts, though it will capture some data if worn for cycling.

DO NOT SWIM while wearing your FuelBand. The device is water resistant, but NOT waterproof.

Is the FuelBand waterproof?

NO. It is water resistant, but it should not be submerged.

Points

How do I earn points?

When you input your FuelScore, you earn points for your physical activity, as well as a daily input bonus. The points you earn from your fuel score are calculated as follows:

FuelScore Credits Points
First 2000 FuelScore 1pt/100 FuelScore
2001st-3000th FuelScore 1pt/50 FuelScore
3001th+ FuelScore 1pt/25 FuelScore

For example, if today's FuelScore is 2500, you will earn 20 points for your first 2000 FuelScore, and 10 points for your next 500, for a total of 30 points. Your points round up.

Each day that you input your FuelScore, you receive a daily input bonus, which varies in value according to your status.

How do I use points I have earned?

Use your points to play the game and earn prizes!

Input Activity

Where do I find my daily steps, calories, and FuelScore?

Every day, insert your FuelBand into the USB port of your computer and connect to the Internet. The first time you connect your FuelBand, follow the instructions on the box to set up your account. From then on, your data will upload automatically to NikePlus.com.

On NikePlus.com, you can view your daily activity. Manually input the data for each day at GSBurnCals.stanford.edu so that you can earn points and win prizes!

When can I input daily activity?

You can input each day's activity any time after 12:00 midnight on the next day.

How many times can I input data each day?

YOu can only input data for each day once. However, you can input data for several calendar days in one sitting. For example, On Feb 20, you can inptu data for Feb 17, 18 and 19.

How do I earn an input bonus?

Each day you enter data, you will earn up to 50 points for inputting data. However, you can onyl receive this input bonus once each day. To maximize receipt of the bonus, upload your data daily, and do not wait to upload several days' data at once.

How much is my input bonus?

The value of your input bonus depends on your status

If your status is... Your input bonus will be...
Bronze 10pts
Silver 30pts
Gold 50pts

Status

How is my status computed?

On any given day, your status is Bronze, Silver, or Gold, determined by your status score. Your status score increases every time you input your FuelScore by the amount you input. For example, when you input a FuelScore of 2500, your status score increases by 2500. Your status is calculated as follows:

If your status score is... Your status is...
0-2000 Bronze
2000-3000 Silver
3001+ Gold

Every day, your status score will drop continuously over the course of the day. The total status points lost each day varies according to your status at that moment.

If your status is... Your status score drops by...
Bronze 2200/day
Silver 2600/day
Gold 3000/day

You status score will never drop below 0 or rise above 5000.

Game

What are the scales?

You can choose to play any of the three scales on the game board. The right side of each scale is weighed down by an unknown amount of cash. Players add points to the scale to weigh down the left side. Each 10 points are equivalent to 5 cents. Once the dollar equivalent of the points on the left side of the scale equals the cash value on the right side of the scale, the scale tips and the player who added the last point wins all of the cash. The winning player will only spend just enough points to tip the scale; no extra points will be taken. Once a scale has tipped, a new one replaces it and the game starts over.

How much cash is likely to be on the right side of the scale?

The probability that the amount of cash on the right side of the scale is any given amount is uniform between $2 and $20. Thus, a $20 prize is just as likely to occur as a $2 prize. Over the course of the quarter, a few prizes between $20 and $100 will be given out.

When do I get my prizes?

At the end of the quarter, you can cash out your rewards with Coupa Cafe Card.

Privacy

Who can see my data?

Only you, your friends on the website, Prof. Prabhakar and Tom Yue can see the data you upload. Your data will be used for statistical analysis to report general trends, but no individually identifiable data will be shared.
